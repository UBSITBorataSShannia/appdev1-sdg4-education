Image Background for Hero Section
===================================

The about.component.css has been updated to reference 'assets/images/hero-background.jpg'

Steps to complete:
1. Save the provided image (children with SDG goals) as: hero-background.jpg
2. Place it in: public/assets/images/

The image will automatically be displayed as the background for the hero section (top part with "Quality Education" title).
The background will NOT extend beyond the "Our Mission" section due to the section structure.

The CSS overlay (rgba with 0.75 opacity) ensures text readability over the image.
