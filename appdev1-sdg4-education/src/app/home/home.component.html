Key Targets by 2030

4.1

Free primary & secondary education

Ensure all girls and boys complete free, equitable, and quality primary and secondary education.

4.2

Early childhood development

Ensure access to quality early childhood development, care, and pre-primary education for all children.

4.3

Technical & vocational access

Ensure equal access for all to affordable and quality technical, vocational, and tertiary education.

4.6

Universal literacy & numeracy

Ensure all youth and substantial adults achieve literacy and numeracy regardless of gender.
